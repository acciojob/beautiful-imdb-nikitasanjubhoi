Chhava is a historical action drama based on the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, the son of the great Maratha king Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The film highlights his bravery, sacrifices, and struggles to protect the Maratha empire against the Mughals. It explores his journey as a warrior prince, his leadership qualities, and his unwavering dedication to his people and kingdom.
